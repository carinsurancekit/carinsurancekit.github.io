How Farmers Insurance Helps Military Families Save Money

  1. Home
  2. Blog
  3. Blog Details

Military families embody resilience, sacrifice, and dedication. They face unique challenges, from frequent relocations and deployments to the constant concern for a loved one's safety. In the midst of these pressures, financial stability should be the last thing they need to worry about. Yet, navigating the complexities of insurance—auto, home, renters, and life—can be a daunting and expensive task. Farmers Insurance, with a long-standing commitment to serving those who serve, offers a suite of tailored products, discounts, and services designed specifically to alleviate the financial burden on military families, helping them save significant money and gain invaluable peace of mind.

The Unique Financial Landscape for Military Families

Understanding the specific pain points of military life is key to appreciating how targeted insurance solutions can make a difference.

Frequent Permanent Change of Station (PCS) Moves

The average military family moves every two to three years. Each PCS move involves transporting vehicles, shipping household goods, and securing new housing, whether on-base or off-base. Each move represents a potential risk to personal property and vehicles, and each new state might come with different insurance regulations and costs. A standard insurance policy might not account for this transient lifestyle, potentially leading to coverage gaps or overpayment.

Deployments and Long-Term Absences

When a service member is deployed, their personal vehicle is often stored for months. Similarly, a family might move in with relatives during a deployment, leaving their primary residence vacant or rented out. Standard auto insurance policies aren't designed for long-term storage, and homeowners insurance can be voided or become more expensive if a property is vacant for an extended period. Paying full price for coverage on assets that aren't in use is a significant and unnecessary financial drain.

The High Cost of Protecting Specialized Assets

Many military families invest in recreational vehicles, boats, or other specialty items to make the most of their limited time together. Insuring these assets can be costly. Furthermore, the valuable personal effects a service member might acquire, such as specialized equipment or high-value electronics, require adequate protection that doesn't break the bank.

Farmers Insurance's Salute to Service: More Than Just a Discount

Farmers goes beyond a simple percentage-off approach. They have developed a multi-faceted program that acknowledges the entirety of the military experience, offering savings and flexibility where it matters most.

Substantial Military Discounts

The cornerstone of their offering is a straightforward and appreciable discount. Active duty, retired military personnel, and veterans can often qualify for a special discount on their auto and other insurance policies. This immediate reduction in premium is a direct and effective way to lower monthly expenses. It’s a simple gesture of thanks that translates into real, tangible savings year after year.

Storage Discount for Deployed Heroes

This is a critically important benefit that directly addresses a major financial pain point. If a service member is deployed and stores their vehicle for more than 30 days, Farmers offers a special storage discount on their comprehensive auto coverage. While the vehicle is in storage, the liability coverage—which is legally mandated for driving—can be reduced, significantly lowering the premium. This ensures the vehicle remains protected from fire, theft, or other damages while stored, but the family isn’t paying for driving coverage that isn't being used. This can result in savings of hundreds of dollars over the course of a deployment.

Tailored Homeowners and Renters Insurance

Whether a family lives in base housing, rents an apartment off-base, or owns a home, Farmers has flexible options. * For Renters: Renters insurance is notoriously affordable and absolutely essential. It protects a family's personal property from theft, fire, or other damage. For a low monthly cost, military families can ensure that their belongings are protected during a move or while in temporary housing. Farmers offers competitive rates and discounts to make this crucial coverage even more accessible. * For Homeowners: For those who purchase a home, Farmers understands that a PCS move might force them to become landlords. They offer guidance and insurance products to help cover a property that is being rented out. Conversely, if a family moves and leaves their home vacant while they secure new housing, they can work with their Farmers agent to adjust the policy appropriately to avoid lapses or excessive costs.

Life Insurance for Unmatched Peace of Mind

The need for robust life insurance is acutely understood within the military community. Farmers offers a variety of term and whole life insurance policies that can supplement Servicemembers' Group Life Insurance (SGLI). This allows families to build a comprehensive financial safety net tailored to their long-term goals, such as paying off a mortgage, funding children's education, or ensuring spousal income. Farmers agents help compare coverage to ensure families are not over-insured or under-insured, optimizing their financial investment.

Leveraging Technology and Expert Guidance for Maximum Savings

Farmers empowers military families with tools and human expertise to manage their policies efficiently and find additional savings.

The Farmers® Mobile App

For a family dealing with a deployment or a PCS move, convenience is king. The Farmers mobile app allows policyholders to: * Access digital ID cards and proof of insurance instantly. * View policy details and make payments from anywhere in the world. * Start and track claims 24/7, a vital feature for families in different time zones. * Contact their agent directly. This seamless management prevents administrative headaches and helps avoid late fees or policy lapses.

The Role of a Knowledgeable Farmers Agent

This is perhaps the greatest asset. A local Farmers agent acts as a personal financial advisor for insurance needs. They take the time to understand a family's specific situation: * Upcoming Move? They can help bundle auto and renters policies for the new location, often unlocking a multi-policy discount. * Planning for Deployment? They will proactively discuss the auto storage discount and guide the family through the process. * Concerned about Coverage Gaps? They conduct regular policy reviews to ensure coverage keeps pace with life changes, ensuring families aren’t paying for what they don’t need and are fully covered for what they do.

Real-World Impact: Saving Money for a More Secure Future

The cumulative effect of these programs is profound. Consider the example of the Smith family: Captain Smith is an Air Force pilot preparing for a year-long deployment. Before he leaves, he meets with his Farmers agent. The agent helps him adjust his auto policy, activating the storage discount and saving the family over $800 for the deployment year. The agent also reviews their renters insurance for the base housing they live in, confirming they have adequate coverage for their personal electronics and furniture during his absence. The military discount already applied saves them 10% annually. Finally, they discuss increasing Captain Smith's life insurance to complement his SGLI, ensuring his family's long-term financial plan remains on track. Through these deliberate, informed actions, the Smith family saves money, optimizes their coverage, and gains the confidence to face the challenges of the coming year.

For military families, every dollar saved is a step toward a more secure financial future—a future where they can focus on what truly matters: supporting their service member and building a strong family life amidst the unique demands of military service. Farmers Insurance, through its dedicated discounts, flexible policies, and personalized agent service, provides a powerful, practical partnership in achieving that goal. It’s a commitment to honoring their service not just with words, but with action and tangible value.
